The First Principles

Why do global travelers still carry physical cash across oceans to buy a meal? Why must a European executive navigate 4 banking handshakes across 3 time zones just to pay for a taxi in Bangkok?

The current financial system is horizontally fragmented. Adding more bank accounts or apps is not the answer. The answer is a vertical leap.

Paycif reduces the multi-step friction of global travel capital into a single, light-speed scan. We accelerate the transition to a borderless Thai economy by solving the fundamental physics of the payment rail.

The Uncontested Ocean

The world doesn't need another generic digital wallet. While regional giants dominate the Asian wallet landscape, a massive segment of global travelers remains underserved.

We explicitly empower those outside the Alipay and WeChat ecosystems—high-value professionals reliant on global credit networks and digital assets who currently lack a native bridge to Thailand's PromptPay network. We are building a unique monopoly on this unaddressed capital flow.

Protocol Integrity

Complexity is the enemy of security. We believe in radical transparency and insanely simple UX. Our sustenance comes from a clear 1.5% protocol fee and a transparent FX spread—calculated at the moment of top-up to ensure zero hidden leakage.

Dedicated to engineering excellence, Paycif is more than a tool: it is an institutional commitment to the future of international settlement.
