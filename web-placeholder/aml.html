AML/KYC Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Commitment to Anti-Money Laundering

Paycif is committed to maintaining the highest standards of Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorist Financing (CTF) compliance. We operate in accordance with global financial standards and the regulations of the Bank of Thailand and AMLO.

2. Identity Verification (KYC)

To prevent illicit activities, we enforce a strict identity verification process:

  • Prior to utilizing high-volume payment bridging, users must undergo identity verification.
  • Verification requires a valid government-issued ID (Passport, National ID) and a biometric liveness check.
  • Corporate accounts require ultimate beneficial owner (UBO) declarations and company registry documents.

3. Monitoring, Reporting, and Restrictions

We employ automated risk-scoring and transaction monitoring systems. Any detected anomalies, structured transactions, or attempted illicit transfers will result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to the relevant Financial Intelligence Units (FIUs). Additionally:

  • Strict Mule Account Prohibition: We strictly prohibit the use of "mule" or "nominee" accounts. Any user found facilitating payments for third parties without authorization will be permanently banned and reported to the Bank of Thailand and AMLO.
  • We actively screen all users against global sanction lists (e.g., OFAC, UN, EU).
  • We do not provide services to residents or citizens of comprehensively sanctioned jurisdictions.
  • High-risk transactions are subject to manual review by our compliance team.

4. Law Enforcement Requests

Paycif cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies and responds promptly to valid subpoenas, court orders, and official requests for information regarding potentially illicit activities.

5. Suspicious Activity Reporting (SAR)

We are obligated to monitor for and report suspicious activity. If a transaction appears suspicious, we reserve the right to freeze the funds and file a SAR with the relevant authorities without prior notice to the user.
