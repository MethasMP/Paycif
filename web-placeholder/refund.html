Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 25, 2026

1. Settlement Finality

Due to the irreversible nature of the PromptPay network and blockchain-based settlements, all successfully executed transactions are considered final. Once liquidity has been bridged and the destination merchant has received the THB settlement, the transaction cannot be reversed or refunded by Paycif.

2. Failed Transactions

In the event of a technical protocol failure where your source funds are debited but the internal bridge to the destination PromptPay settlement fails to execute, Paycif guarantees a full refund of the original principal amount. The refund will be initiated automatically to the original payment method or wallet within 24 to 48 business hours.

3. Service and Gas Fees

Applicable Paycif processing fees and underlying blockchain network gas fees are strictly non-refundable once a transaction has been successfully broadcasted, regardless of any subsequent dispute between the payer and the merchant.

4. Merchant Disputes

Paycif is solely a payment facilitation infrastructure. We do not manufacture, sell, or deliver any physical or digital goods purchased from third-party Thai merchants. Any disputes regarding the quality, non-delivery, or condition of goods and services must be resolved directly with the merchant in question. Paycif cannot perform chargebacks on completed PromptPay transactions.

5. Requesting an Investigation

If you believe a transaction was executed fraudulently without your authorization, or a failed transaction has not been automatically refunded, you may open an investigation ticket within 7 days of the incident by contacting our support team.
