Got Questions?

Do I need a Thai bank account?
No. Paycif acts as your digital settlement bridge. Corporate users can fund their accounts via international wires or digital assets, and we settle with Thai merchants via PromptPay instantly.
Where is PromptPay accepted?
Everywhere. From 7-Eleven and malls to street food vendors and island taxis. If they have a QR code, you can pay.
Is it safe?
Completely. We use AES-256 encryption and follow PCI-DSS standards. We never store your card details on our servers.
What are the fees?
We provide the mid-market exchange rate with a tiny, transparent processing fee that is significantly lower than ATM withdrawal charges.
